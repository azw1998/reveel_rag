Document ID: premodel_join_surcharge_exact_matches
Document Type: join
Databricks Catalog: prod
Left Table Name: premodel
Right Table Name: exact_matches

Description:
The premodel table does not have surcharge id. 
When we want to know about surcharge info from the premodel table we can't just filter on charge_description.
We have to join exact_matches onto premodel to get the surcharge_id and surcharge_name, and then use the surcharge_name to answer the initial query.
